Contingent Leader Registration Form

The contingent leader of a college takes care of the accommodation of all the teams and visitors coming from that particular college. Anyone who wants to avail accommodation must contact their contingent leader. A contingent leader is selected from each and every college and will guide you through the accommodation process during your stay at IIT Bombay. He/she will bridge the gap between you and Mood Indigo.
