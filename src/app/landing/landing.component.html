About Us

IIT Bombay’s Mood Indigo is Asia’s Largest College Cultural Festival

Best Four Days Of Your Life!

It annually entertains a footfall of more than 1,43,000 students from over 1700 colleges spanning across India. Over the years, Mood Indigo has played host to lots of artists, celebrities and events spanning 20+ genres.

It's a melting pot of Indian and Global culture and a showcase of spectacular talent - where streets come alive and every step you take is a step towards new experience, where you can never see it all, but what you do see last lifetime. It’s a little bubble in space and time of infinite possibilities, the place to be in December; and in short: the best four days of your life.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mood Indigo College Connect Program about?

Mood Indigo College Connect Program is a student connect program that aims to build a strong network of culturally enthusiastic and active students across India.

How do I apply? What happens after I apply?

You can apply directly through our website. Once the selection process is completed, you will get notified if you are selected.

What is my responsibility being a part of the program?

As a Part of Mood Indigo team, you are required to do the following Ideate on and execute innovative events plan Work actively for social media publicity Strategize and drive Word of Mouth publicity campaigns in campus events and activities

Who can apply?

Students from all colleges across the Globe are welcome to apply.

What are the incentives for being a part of the College Connect Program?

This is your opportunity to develop strategic, communication and leadership skills and get rewarded for your work. Apart from the learning experience involved that will benefit your career, you will also get incentives like Mood Indigo exclusive merchandise & accessories, VR Headsets, Speakers & Headphones, Nivea Goodies, and many more prizes. Top students also stand a chance to intern at best companies in India.

Who can I contact if I have any questions regarding the college connect program?

You can contact us at publicrelations@moodi.org.
